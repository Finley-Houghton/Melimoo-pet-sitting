Welcome to Melimoo

We treat every pet like one of our own

We're a small, client-centred family business built on a lifetime of loving animals. When you're away — whether that's for an hour, a long weekend, or a whole holiday — we come to your home so your pets stay relaxed in the place they know best, surrounded by their own beds, smells and routines.

Feeding, fresh water, walks, playtime, cuddles and proper company — we do it all, and we do it with a smile. No kennels, no stress, no strangers — just genuine, attentive care from people who truly love what they do. Your pet is part of a family, and that's exactly how we treat them.

Meet your sitter

Say hello to Mel

Mel has spent over three years pet sitting a wonderful range of animals — everything from boisterous dogs to gentle rabbits. Having grown up in a family of farmers, she even has hands-on farm experience and was raised in a wonderfully pet-saturated home, so she's completely comfortable and confident around household animals of every shape and size.

Clients regularly comment on her attention to detail — keeping on top of dietary needs, exercise routines and the individual little touches that make each pet feel safe and happy. No matter the animal, Mel believes every pet is a member of a family, and gets all the love and attention they deserve when she's around.
